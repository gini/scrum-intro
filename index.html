What's Scrum?

  • An empirical, iterative, and incremental agile software development framework for managing software projects
source: Wikipedia
  • Product Owner: represents stakeholders and is the voice of the customer; accountable for ensuring the team delivers value to the business; writes customer-centric items, prioritizes them, adds them to the product backlog.
  • Development Team: self-organizing; responsible for delivering potentially shippable product increments at end of Sprint; consists of 3-9 people with cross-functional skills doing the actual work
  • Scrum Master: facilitates Scrum process; "servant-leader"; accountable for removing impediments to the team; ensures that the Scrum process is used as intended; protects the Development Team and keeps it focused on the tasks at hand
